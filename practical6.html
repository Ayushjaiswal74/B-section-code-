Michael Jackson, the King of Pop, was one of the most influential artists in music history. His contributions to music, dance, and fashion made a lasting impact on pop culture. From hits like "Billie Jean" and "Thriller" to his legendary moonwalk, his legacy continues to inspire generations.

Born on August 29, 1958, Michael Jackson revolutionized the music industry with his extraordinary talent. His humanitarian work and love for fans further cemented his place as a global icon.
